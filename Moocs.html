MOOCs - A wonderful way to learn

I discovered Coursera and EdX at the beginning of 2014 and my learning journey has never been the same again. Having access to course material from the best universities in the world on any pretty much topic is a great blessing.

I find it difficult to resist signing up for a course that I find intersting and then I agonize for the next 6-8 weeks trying to keep up with the course load, assignment, quizes and finals - all while keeping a full-time job. At any given point in time I'm signed up for 4-5 courses and keep praying that my wife/kids don't disown me for getting sucked into my MOOC pursuits!
